My Book Reviews

Evelyn Louie

Hello! This site is to record my reviews and ratings of books I have read! Below you will find a table that includes the titles, authors, ratings and reasonings of each book. Below them I included some of my favourite pictures or cover arts from said books.

Book Title Author Rating Reasonings Behind My Choices (Pros and Cons)
Miss Peregrine's Home for Peculiar Children Ransom Riggs 4 out of 5 This book has a very unique and interesting concept. I really enjoyed how things progressed in this first book. The romance with the main character was a little off putting. There was also a few times where points or logic in the book contradicted each other.
The Selection Series Kierra Cass 3 out of 5 These books were quite... interesting. The covers are stunning and I really loved the side characters. Things progressed oddly and some things did not make sense. I did not like the ending very much.
Cinder Marissa Meyer 5 out of 5 Love love love this take on Cinderella. Another unique premise and really love how it changes the details of a classic fairy tale. A bit predictable at points.
